Our Departments

Cardiology Department

Specialized in diagnosing and treating heart diseases. State-of-the-art cardiac care facility.

Pediatrics Department

Comprehensive care for children. Focuses on growth, development, and childhood illnesses.

Expert care for musculoskeletal system. Specializes in joint replacements and sports injuries.

Advanced diagnosis and treatment of nervous system disorders. Cutting-edge neurological care.

Mental health assessments and therapies designed to support emotional well-being and treat various psychiatric disorders.
