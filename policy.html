Hospital Policies and Rules

Welcome to Guru Healthcare Center. To ensure a safe and positive experience for all patients and visitors, we have established the following policies and rules:

Patient Rights

  • Every patient has the right to receive considerate, respectful, and compassionate care.
  • Patients have the right to participate in decisions regarding their care.
  • Patients have the right to confidentiality and privacy regarding their medical records.

Visiting Hours

Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We request that visitors adhere to these hours to ensure a peaceful environment for our patients.

Infection Control

  • Please use hand sanitizer upon entering the facility.
  • Visitors experiencing symptoms of illness (fever, cough, etc.) are advised to postpone their visit.
  • Only two visitors per patient are allowed in patient rooms at any given time.

Conduct Expectations

  • All visitors must behave in a respectful and courteous manner.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited within hospital premises.
  • Weapons and illegal substances are not allowed on hospital property.

Patient Responsibilities

  • Patients are expected to provide accurate information about their health history.
  • Patients should inform their healthcare provider of any changes in their condition.
  • Patients are responsible for following the treatment plan agreed upon with their healthcare provider.

Emergency Procedures

In case of an emergency, please call +91 958 742 3654 or notify a staff member immediately. Our facility has protocols in place to respond to emergencies effectively.

Thank you for choosing Guru Healthcare Center. We are committed to providing you with high-quality care and support.
