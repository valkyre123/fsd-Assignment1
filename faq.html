Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does Guru Healthcare Center offer?

We offer a variety of services, including general check-ups, specialized treatments, emergency care, and surgical procedures.
2. How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our front desk at +91 955 079 3200 or by visiting our Contact Us page.
3. What are the visiting hours for patients?

Our visiting hours are from 9 AM to 8 PM, daily. Please check with the respective departments for any specific visiting hours.
4. Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept various health insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more information.
5. What should I bring for my first appointment?

For your first appointment, please bring your ID, insurance card, and any relevant medical records.
6. Are there any emergency services available?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent medical attention.
7. How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We welcome feedback from our patients. You can provide feedback through our Contact Us page or by filling out a feedback form available at the reception.
