Policy

At Guru Healthcare Center, we prioritize the safety and security of our patients, staff, and visitors. Our policies encompass patient identification protocols, restrictions on unauthorized access, and strict measures against harassment or disruptive behavior. Additionally, we encourage our patients and their families to report any concerns or suspicious activities to our staff immediately.
